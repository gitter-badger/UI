Built on Bootstrap

lymUI is being built from the source of Bootstrap. This makes it easy to learn and fast to use.

Clean

Aiming for a simple look, lymUI removes all unnecessary styles from Bootstrap.

Time saving

lymUI comes with many prebuilt webpages coded in Jade, saving you work.
